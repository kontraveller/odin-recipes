Boeuf Bourguignon

Beef with a red wine sauce

Ingredients (serves 4):

  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp ground black pepper
  • 2 pounds cubed stew meat
  • 1/4 cup butter
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 carrots, chopped
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • 2 cups red wine
  • 1 bay leaf
  • 3 tbs chopped fresh parsley
  • ½ tsp dried thyme
  • 1 (6 ounce) can sliced mushrooms
  • 1 (16 ounce) canned onions

Steps:

  1. Combine flour, salt and pepper in a small bowl.
    Coat the beef cubes with this mixture
  2. Melt the butter in a large skillet over a medium high heat.
    Add the meat and brown well on all sides.
    Pour into a 2 quart casserole dish
  3. Return the skillet to the heat and add the onion, carrots and garlic to it.
    Saute for 5 to 10 mins. or until onion is tender.
    Add wine, bay leaf, parsley, thyme and liquid from the mushrooms. Pour over meat
  4. Bake, covered at 175 degrees C for 2½ hours.
    Remove cover, add canned onions and mushroom crowns and bake for 30 mins.
